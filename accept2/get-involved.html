Pray

“The prayer of a righteous man is powerful and effective.”

James 5:16 (NIV)

We request all your prayers for the work that the Lord has assigned to us. Pray that we may do his work honestly, faithfully, and to the best of our capabilities. Pray for the patients that they may come to know the Lord and accept him. We also request prayers for our staff, that the Lord may strengthen them and help them serve him fully.

 

Volunteer

It is possible to come visit us either alone or as a group and help us out in our day-to-day activities. We get a good number of people visiting us and helping us out. We give the volunteering group a small briefing session to help them understand HIV/AIDS, dispel any myths, and clear doubts.

Please contact us if you with to volunteer alone or as a group.

 

Support

If you are burdened by the work that we do, you can support us financially and help us in our ministry.

Kindly make  donations only in the name of ACCEPT and payable at Bangalore. All Indian contributions to ACCEPT are eligible for Tax Exemption from Income Tax under Income Tax clause 80(G). ACCEPT also has FCRA permission to receive funds in foreign currency.

ACCEPT is managed by a Board and all the accounts are annually audited by a reputed Chartered Accountancy Firm.
