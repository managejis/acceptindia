Home About Us

ACCEPT (AIDS Counselling, Care, Education, Prevention, and Training) is located beside Doddagubbi, a small village off Hennur Road in Bangalore. It gives inpatient care for up to 25 patients at a time and to many more as outpatients. The Care Centre has become the hub of ACCEPT's activities. ACCEPT Society strives to meet the medical, social, psychological, economic, and spiritual needs of the HIV infected in India. Based out of Bangalore, Karnataka, ACCEPT is today recognized by NACO (Govt. of India) as the only A-Grade Care Centre of its kind in Bangalore, where the prevalence of HIV infected is comparatively high.

The Society was instituted in 1999 by Raju K Mathew and a team of like-minded children of God as a response to the plight of HIV/AIDS patients, to accept them and show the love of Jesus through the words and deeds as an outreach of a local church.

In the last 8 years of its service, ACCEPT cared and offered hope to hundreds of HIV/AIDS infected and families affected by them. ACCEPT has helped many regain they health, find a home when abandoned, and die with dignity. HIV orphans also find a caring home in ACCEPT. Above all, a number of them now life with us and leave us with eternal hope.
