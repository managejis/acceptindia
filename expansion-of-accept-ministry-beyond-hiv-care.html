Expansion of Accept ministry beyond HIV care

Posted by on September 28, 2014   |  No Comments

Accept ministry is now prayerfully planning to expand the ministry beyond HIV care to also run a small old age home for 8 needy ones. Many of the existing homes for such are very costly for many of the needy ones. We have already taken three such needy ones but we do not have the proper infrastructure. We have a need to modify a building that we have to make it fit for the elderly care with proper bathrooms and other needed facilities. This has a need of about Rs. 400,000 (4 lakhs). If the Lord exercises your heart to partner with us, kindly contact us by email or phone. rajujoyce@gmail.com /91 9448619619. Your inputs and prayers are very valuable.
