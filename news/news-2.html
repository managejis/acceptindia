Nagpur Centre Handed Over

Posted by rajukm on October 21, 2011  |   No Comments »

ACCEPT started a small extension service in Nagpur in 2009, due to the request of a brother and many patients. We have been managing the ministry from Bangalore with the help of an advisory committee and a small staff team of five people. Over these years, it has helped over 1000 patients and their families. We were also able to share the love of Christ to all who came through our deeds and word.

Since it was difficult to manage the ministry from Bangalore, we were looking for some assembly to take over the managing and running of the centre. Christian Brethren Assembly, Civil Lines, Nagpur has come forward and we have decided to turn the ministry over to the local assembly from Nov. 1st 2011 onwards. So kindly pray for the local assembly who is taking this responsibility and extend all the support to carry on the ministry.

Pray for the Land issue

Posted by rajukm on July 14, 2011  |   2 Comments »

We bought the existing land from a local person with all documents. But now Govt. is claiming that part of the land is theirs and has given us notice to vacate. This will have major implication on the ministry  and will affect the work of children home and hospice. We are approaching the govt. Kindly pray for solution that will be beneficial to the ministry. We may need to go to court and get a stay order. Please pray that God would guide us in this issue.
