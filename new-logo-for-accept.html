New Logo for ACCEPT

Posted by admin on July 6, 2010  |   No Comments »

ACCEPT has completed 10 years! Praise the Lord! To celebrate this blessed success we thought it would be appropriate to create a new logo.
An advertising agency volunteered to design the new logo for us and we released the new logo during the opening of the Children home.
The new logo expresses the love that God spreads in our hearts. We continueÂ  to share the same love for others, especially those who are affected with HIV/AIDS which are represented by the red ribbon.
