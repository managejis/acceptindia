Nagpur Centre

You are here: Home » What We Do » Nagpur Centre

In May 2009 ACCEPT opened an  Out Patient Clinic/Day Care Centre in Nagpur in Maharashtra State.

Within one year more than 149 HIV/AIDS patients received care at our OPC/Day Care Centre, which is in a rented building. Now we are blessed to have a part time doctor, two nurses and three administrative staff to take care of the needs in the centre.

Besides OPC facility, the Outreach/Home based care team of ACCEPT Nagpur visited more than 265 families who are affected with HIV/AIDS. While visiting homes the ACCEPT Outreach/Home Care team provided the family members with home based care.
