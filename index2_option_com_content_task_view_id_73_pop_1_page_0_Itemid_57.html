Action points for the Church
  1. Mobilise prayer. It is effective.
  2. Provide a climate of love, acceptance and support for those who are vulnerable to, or affected by HIV/AIDS.
  3. Reversing the discrimination and building supportive environments by organising Bible studies, Cell groups and support groups for infected and affected by HIV/AIDS.
  4. Holistic Care – mental, emotional, physical and spiritual care and support of people living with HIV/AIDS. Encourage your congregation to involve in their lives through visitations, prayer, sharing resources etc
  5. Acknowledging the calling of those individuals who are responding to this disaster, as true vocations under God and be willing to see is truly at work in their lives.

n Encourage faithfulness and refraining from risky behaviour as the key to avoid HIV/AIDS. Promoting family values and abstinence from sex until marriage.

n Encourage the church to seek and understand more fully the gift of human sexuality in the context of personal responsibility, family and Christian faith.

n Ignorance is not bliss, it costs lives. Promoting and sharing of accurate information about HIV/AIDS and a climate of open discussion does work against the spread of misinformation and fear.

n Encourage people living with HIV/AIDS (PLWHAs) to involve actively in church activities.
